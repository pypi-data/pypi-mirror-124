Luna

Luna is a web client for lotide.

What is lotide?

lotide is an attempt to build a federated forum. Users can create communities to share links and text posts and discuss them with other users, include those registered on other servers through ActivityPub.

Why Luna?

In Latin, luna means moon, the celestial body that control tides.

I wrote Luna for several reasons, some are personal such as wanting to play with Django, some are less personal like wanting an alternative client that looks pretty on many screen size (by the time I achieve this goal, hitide, the official frontend, will already have resolved the device problem, though) or wanting a Rust-free, JavaScript-free client that's self-hostable.
